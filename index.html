Biography

Zengyang Gong is currently Research Assistant at Beijing Normal University - Hong Kong Baptist University United International College(UIC), where he works with Prof. Weijia Jia. Before joining UIC, he worked as Research Intern under Dr. Bo Du's supervision at University of Wollongong, Australia from 09/2019 to 01/2020. He received the B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Wuhan University of Science and Technology in June 2017, and the Master degree in Computer Technology from Shenzhen University under Dr. Zhidan Liu's supervision in June 2020.

Research Interests

Networked and Distributed Sensing, Mobile and Pervasive Computing, Big Data Analytics, and Urban Computing
