About

I'm Zengyang Gong, A Research Software Engineer in the RSES Group at the University of Sheffield; and a staff PhD Candidate in the Visual Computing research group of the Department of Computer Science at the University of Sheffield, working under the supervision of Dr Paul Richmond and Dr Steve Maddock.

My research is focussed on improving the run-time performance of road network simulation using many-core architectures, such as Graphics Processing Units (GPUs).
